How to Protect Yourself

There are many steps you can take to reduce your risk of infection from the COVID-19 virus and reduce the risk of spreading it to others. WHO and CDC recommend following these precautions:

Wash your
hands frequently

Maintain social
distancing

Avoid touching eyes,
nose and mouth

Prevention

To prevent infection and to slow transmission of COVID-19, do the following:

  1. 1. Get vaccinated when a vaccine is available to you.
  2. 2. Stay at least 1 metre apart from others, even if they don’t appear to be sick.
  3. 3. Wear a properly fitted mask when physical distancing is not possible or when in poorly ventilated settings.
  4. 4. Choose open, well-ventilated spaces over closed ones. Open a window if indoors.
  5. 5. Wash your hands regularly with soap and water or clean them with alcohol-based hand rub.
  6. 6. Cover your mouth and nose when coughing or sneezing.
  7. 7. If you feel unwell, stay home and self-isolate until you recover.
